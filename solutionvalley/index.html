PROJECT: 6POINT6 – CYBER RESILIENCE

OVERVIEW

Bottling up the expertise within the 6point6 cyber team we have created a self assessment cyber resilience tool. This enables businesses to evaluate and gain meaningful insights and actions that they can take to improve their resilience to cyber events. Bullet have been behind the brand, positioning, strategy and product design and build.

PROJECT: OPEN BORDERS DIRECT

OVERVIEW

UK businesses trade over £800bn worth of goods each year, yet many do not have access to key information and products necessary to enable international trade with ease, speed and safety. We carried out an in-depth digital discovery, the results of which were validated with user research with UK businesses. Using this insight , we created the vision for a new, innovative digital service solving the challenges faced by businesses when trading internationally.

PROJECT: 6POINT6 – 360

OVERVIEW

Utilising graph technology, Client 360 is a data intelligence product that provides wealth & asset managers insights into their clients’ relationships, proximity to hot topics and mapping live sales opportunities. Bullet have been responsible for all aspects of the brand, creation of the proposition, strategy, product design and build.
